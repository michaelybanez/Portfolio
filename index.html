About Me

Designing products that work — not just products that look like they work.

I started as a graphic designer and spent six years building brands, campaigns, and visual systems for clients across five industries. That work taught me how design holds up — or doesn't — when it moves from screen to print, from concept to production, from approval to build.

Along the way I moved into UI/UX and frontend, not as a separate track but as a natural extension of the same discipline. I know how digital interfaces work, how design systems are built, and how HTML and CSS interpret a design file. That background shapes how I approach every brief — print or digital.

Based in Cebu, Philippines. Available for remote collaboration. Work best with organizations that recognize design as a driver of value and impact—not just an aesthetic layer, but a strategic tool for achieving business goals.

Let's build something worth shipping.
